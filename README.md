# Blinkit_Dashboard
 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer ratings, and product category insights. It helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.
The dashboard enables users to analyze sales, ratings, and outlet performance across different outlet sizes, outlet types, and item categories through interactive visualizations and filters.

🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard
The primary purpose of this dashboard is to:
Monitor overall sales performance.
Analyze customer ratings across product categories.
Compare outlet performance based on size and location type.
Identify high-performing and low-performing product categories.
Support strategic business decisions using real-time insights.
Improve customer satisfaction by understanding rating trends.

🚀 Business Problems Addressed
1. Lack of Visibility into Sales Performance
Management needs a centralized view of sales metrics across products and outlets.

2. Understanding Customer Satisfaction
Identifying which products and outlet types receive better customer ratings.

3. Outlet Performance Comparison
Comparing performance across outlet sizes and locations to optimize business strategy.

4. Product Portfolio Optimization
Determining which product categories perform well and which require improvement.

5. Data-Driven Decision Making
Providing actionable insights to support marketing, inventory, and operational decisions.
